Welcome to eventpride!

Welcome to eventpride - your ultimate destination for discovering and hosting exhilarating online and in-person events throughout California, USA. Created by the talented minds of Aaron Pride and Fernanda Murillo, EventPride was born as a scholar project with a mission to bring people together, celebrate diverse passions, and ignite unforgettable experiences. Inspired by the renowned platform Eventbrite, we take pride in our user-friendly design and cutting-edge technology, making event organization seamless for both hosts and attendees.

At eventpride, we believe in the power of connection and the joy of shared moments. Our platform is a celebration of creativity, innovation, and community-driven experiences. From electrifying concerts to enriching workshops, we curate a wide array of events to cater to every interest and taste. Join us on this thrilling journey of discovery and empowerment. Whether you're a seasoned event organizer or a curious attendee, EventPride is your gateway to a world of endless possibilities.
